How to Use the App

Get started in just four easy steps:

1. Create Your Profile

Download the app and sign up with your basic details so we can personalize your experience.

2. Enter Your Symptoms

Choose from our guided categories (like breathing, coughing, allergies) or type them in directly.

3. Get AI-Backed Guidance

Our AI model, trained in-house under medical supervision, will suggest possible causes and the next steps you can take.

4. Follow Recommendations

If the app suggests urgent care, contact a doctor right away. You can also save your results to track changes over time.
