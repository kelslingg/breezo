Our Approach to Data

Evidence-Based Foundation

Our AI model is trained on publicly available respiratory health datasets and peer-reviewed medical literature. We prioritize data quality and scientific rigor to provide reliable health guidance.

Regional Focus

We specifically incorporate data relevant to respiratory conditions common in Kazakhstan, including seasonal patterns, environmental factors, and local health trends.

Privacy Commitment

No user data is collected or stored. Our model operates entirely on publicly available datasets and research, ensuring complete privacy protection.
