How We Use AI

×

We use AI to make early health guidance faster, more accurate, and more accessible—helping people take the right steps toward care sooner.

Our AI model is developed and trained in-house by our team, under the close supervision of qualified medical professionals. Using verified medical data and insights from healthcare experts, it’s designed to:

  • 1 Analyze symptoms against medically validated patterns for respiratory diseases.
  • 2 Adapt to local health trends to ensure relevant, timely recommendations.
  • 3 Stay reliable through ongoing expert review and model refinement.

Your privacy is our priority. All health information you share is securely stored and never shared without your consent.

While our AI can provide helpful guidance, it is not a substitute for professional medical advice—only a tool to support your health decisions.
